MailMerge

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

100% local — your data never reaches us

MailMerge is a desktop application that runs entirely on your own computer. We, the developers, operate no servers that receive your data. Your contact lists, the emails you write, your attachments and your email-account credentials are processed only on your machine and sent only to the services you choose.

What we collect

Nothing. MailMerge contains no analytics, no telemetry, no tracking, and no “phone-home” of your contacts, messages or usage. We never see your contact list or the content of your emails.

Where your data goes

  • Your contacts & emails stay in files on your computer. They are sent only to your own email provider (the SMTP server you configure) when you press Send — exactly as if you sent the emails yourself.
  • Inbox sync connects directly to your own IMAP inbox using the credentials you enter, to find replies, bounces and unsubscribes. Nothing from your inbox is sent anywhere else.
  • Email-account password is stored locally in your operating system’s secure keychain (macOS Keychain / Windows Credential Manager), encrypted by the OS. It is never transmitted to us.

Network connections the app makes

  • Your email provider (SMTP to send, IMAP to scan) — using credentials you enter.
  • Mail-server auto-detection — to fill in server settings, the app may query Mozilla’s public ISPDB (autoconfig.thunderbird.net) and your email domain’s autoconfig endpoint. Only the domain part of your address is used.
  • Address verification (MX) — public DNS lookups of recipient domains (not full addresses) to check they can receive mail.
  • License activation — when you activate or validate your license, the app contacts Lemon Squeezy (our payment/licensing provider) sending only your license key and an anonymous machine identifier (a one-way hash, not reversible to your identity, your name, or your data).

You are the data controller of your contacts

Because we never receive your contacts, you are the controller of the personal data in your lists. You are responsible for having a lawful basis (e.g. consent or legitimate interest under the GDPR) to email the people on your list, and for honouring unsubscribe requests. MailMerge is a tool for legitimate, personalized outreach to your own contacts — not for unsolicited bulk email.

Your rights

Since we hold none of your personal data, there is nothing for us to access, export or delete. To remove the app’s local data from your computer, uncheck “Remember settings & password” (which clears the saved settings and keychain entry) and delete the application.
