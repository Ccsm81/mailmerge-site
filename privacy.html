MailMerge

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

The short version: MailMerge is a desktop application that runs entirely on your computer. We do not collect, receive, store or sell any of your data. We do not operate servers that your contacts or emails pass through.

What the app does with your data

  • Your contact lists (Excel/CSV files) are read locally and stay on your computer.
  • Your emails are sent directly from your computer through your own email provider (SMTP), exactly like a normal mail program. They never pass through us.
  • Your email credentials (password or app password) are stored, if you choose, in your operating system's secure keychain — on your device only.
  • Statistics (sent, replies, bounces, unsubscribes) are computed locally by reading your own inbox over IMAP, and stored locally.
  • The unsubscribe list is stored locally so the app can respect opt-outs in future sends.

Sign in with Microsoft (Outlook.com)

If you connect an Outlook.com account, authentication happens directly between your computer and Microsoft using the industry-standard OAuth 2.0 flow. The app receives only a sending token, which is stored in your system keychain. We never see your Microsoft password or your token.

What we (the developer) receive

  • Purchases and licensing are processed by Lemon Squeezy, our merchant of record. They handle payment data under their own privacy policy; we receive your order details (name, email, license key) to provide support.
  • License activation: when you activate, the app contacts the Lemon Squeezy licensing API with your license key and an anonymous machine identifier to enforce the one-computer-at-a-time license. No personal files, contacts or email content are transmitted.
  • No analytics, no tracking: the app contains no analytics, telemetry, advertising or tracking of any kind. This website is a static page and sets no cookies.

Data retention & deletion

Because your data lives on your own computer, deleting it is in your hands: remove the app and its local data folder at any time. For order data held by Lemon Squeezy, contact us or exercise your rights directly with Lemon Squeezy.

Your rights (GDPR)

We are based in Romania (EU). Since we process almost no personal data, most requests concern order records. You have the right to access, correct or delete the order data associated with your purchase — email us and we will resolve it promptly.
