Questions, answered

The short version: it's a normal desktop app that sends normal email — just personalized, and a lot faster than doing it by hand.

Is my contact list uploaded anywhere?
No. MailMerge runs entirely on your computer and sends through your own email account. Your list, your messages and your statistics never touch our servers — we don't have servers.
Which email accounts does it work with?
Any account with standard SMTP: your company domain (use your normal password), Gmail and Yahoo (they require an "app password" — the app takes you to the exact page to create one in ~30 seconds), and Outlook.com/Hotmail with one-click Sign in with Microsoft.
Is this a spam tool?
No. MailMerge is built for legitimate, personal outreach to your own contacts — clients, partners, members. It paces your sending, respects a permanent unsubscribe list and keeps everything transparent in your own Sent folder.
On how many computers can I use my license?
One computer at a time. Moving to a new machine is self-service: deactivate in the app on the old one, activate on the new one — as many times as you like.
Mac or Windows?
Both. Your purchase includes the macOS app (Intel & Apple Silicon) and the Windows installer. A Microsoft Store listing is also on its way.
How do I get it after paying?
Instantly. The checkout emails you the download links and your license key the moment payment completes. Install, paste the key, done.
