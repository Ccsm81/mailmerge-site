Frequently asked questions

Is this really a one-time purchase?

Yes. You pay once and own that version forever — no subscription, no recurring charge, no account to manage.

Do you offer refunds?

All sales are final — which is exactly why there’s a free trial: download MailMerge and send up to 50 emails before you buy, so you can confirm it works with your own list and email account first.

Does it work with Outlook / Office 365 / my own domain?

Yes. MailMerge works with any SMTP server — Gmail, Outlook/Office 365, Yahoo, GMX, or your custom domain. Gmail and Yahoo require an app password (not your normal password); MailMerge guides you.

Where does my contact data go?

Nowhere. Everything runs locally on your computer. Your spreadsheet and emails never touch our servers — we don’t have any. Your email password is stored in your operating system’s secure keychain.

How do I avoid landing in spam?

MailMerge sends through your own account, so deliverability depends on your provider and your list. Use the built-in send delay, stay within your provider’s daily limit (e.g. Gmail ~500/day), set up SPF/DKIM on your domain, and only email people who expect to hear from you. MailMerge is for personalized outreach to your own contacts — not cold spam.

What if I get a new computer?

Each license activates on one computer. Click “Deactivate license” in the app on the old machine, then activate on the new one. No limit on how many times you move it.

Mac and Windows?

Both. The macOS build is a universal app (Intel + Apple Silicon). Windows is a standard installer.
